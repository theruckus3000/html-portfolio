The Best Movies According to Javier

My top 3 movies of all time

Funny, witty, with just the right amount of charm to have anyone laughing in their seats.

Carlito's Way

Classic Al Pacino doing what he does best as a gangster trying to go straight in NYC.

The Grand Budapest Hotel

The story telling is suberb and one of the best done on screen in recent times.
